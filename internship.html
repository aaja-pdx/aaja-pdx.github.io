AAJA-SPJ Emerging Northwest Journalists Internship

Update: Applications are open until November 25!

AAJA-Portland and SPJ Oregon Territory Chapter are pleased to announce that OPB will host the AAJA/SPJ’s Emerging Northwest Journalist Internship. Applicants don’t need radio or television experience but will have the opportunity to gain valuable multimedia experience. Additionally, the new deadline to apply is November 25.

AAJA-Portland and SPJ Oregon Territory Chapter are creating a new, paid internship to promote underrepresented voices in Northwest newsrooms, starting in summer 2019.

Applicants must be a college sophomore, junior, senior or graduate student majoring in journalism, or who aspire to a career in journalism Applicants must reside in or attend school in Oregon or Southwest Washington. Applicants who previously lived in Oregon or Southwest Washington will also be considered. Applicants must have a valid driver's license.

The selected intern will be assigned to a Portland-area news organization that will provide a staff mentor for the duration of the 10-week internship. The selected intern will be paid at prevailing regional minimum wage.

This application will require:

  • A cover letter (We welcome your thoughts on any topic, but we would particularly like to read about your experiences with diversity and journalism.)
  • A resume
  • Up to five work samples
  • Up to three references with contact information

Please gather these materials before you begin. You can apply using this form by Sunday, Nov. 18.
